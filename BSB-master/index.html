About Us

About BodySnatchers

After spending 20 years at Poole Amateur Boxing Club as a boxer and then coach, I then spent 7 years at Phoenix MMA as the boxing coach. In all that time I’ve had over 40 bouts. I’m also a British Boxing Board of Control registered professional coach. Now, I’ve started up my own team. BodySnatchers. Whether you want to box competitively, get fit, lose weight, or just enjoy training, BodySnatchers maybe the place for you.
